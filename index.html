Privacy Policy for Detect the Sus

Last updated: 26/12/2024

Policy Scope

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application “Detect the Sus,” available on the Google Play Store, and is intended for all users, including children under the age of 13.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. This includes names, email addresses, device IDs, or any other information that can be used to identify a user.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data. There is no usage of analytics, ads, or external tracking tools.

Children’s Privacy

In compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), this app is safe for children under 13. No data is collected, and no in-app purchases, links, or ads are shown to children.

If a parent or guardian believes we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately at brentN5@outlook.com and we will remove any such information promptly.

Legal Compliance

Although we do not collect data, we strive to comply with applicable privacy laws, including GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California), by ensuring no personal data is gathered, processed, or shared.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
